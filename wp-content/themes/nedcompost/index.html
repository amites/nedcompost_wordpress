Do your part for Zero-Waste and Nederland's Sustainability

Composting is a vital and necessary piece of the movement toward a net-zero carbon nuetral lifestyle in the mountains. If we're going to create a sustainable economy, we have to start and composting is a way to take an important step in the right direction.

Our Faithful Team

The NedCompost Board is composed of die-hard believers in sustainability and in our collective capacity to change the course of history through small local efforts. We understand ourselves to be among an army of revolutionary thinkers and doers who maintain that the impossible is possible. Together with the many volunteers who have contributed to the development of Nederland's homegrown compost program have spent hundreds of hours bootstrapping this fledgling organization into its adolescence. We've done it for love of compost, for the love of our community, for the love of our planet, and the love of beautiful relationship between humanity and our mother earth.

The Town of Nederland, CO has been monumental in helping us to get our operation off the ground so please tell lovely pro-compost folks at Town Hall and on the Nederland Board of Trustees "Thank you for supporting NedCompost" if you have a chance. Also, Boulder County has been very supportive. Also, an army of local and former local volunteers have been helpful and we extend our gratitude to them.

Most of all, THANK YOU, to the NedCompost customers many of whom have been with us since day 1. We couldn't be where we are today without your loyal commitment to composting with us.
